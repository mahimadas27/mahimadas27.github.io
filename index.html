Researching the Psychology of Trauma & Resilience

I'm a psychological researcher focused on understanding the impacts of childhood trauma and developing evidence-based interventions for marginalized populations. My work bridges quantitative research methods with compassionate, trauma-informed applications.

Currently pursuing my Masters in Research in Psychological Methods at the University of Sussex, I'm passionate about creating meaningful change through rigorous scientific inquiry and accessible psychological practice.

About Me

My academic journey in psychology began at the University of Calcutta, where I developed a foundational understanding of human behavior and cognition. This evolved into specialized research in trauma psychology during my MSc at Bengaluru City University, where I graduated 3rd in my class with a CGPA of 8.98.

Beyond academia, I've worked directly with affected populations as a caregiver, remedial educator, and trainee counsellor. These experiences ground my research in real-world applications and inform my commitment to creating interventions that are both scientifically rigorous and culturally sensitive.

When I'm not conducting research, I'm involved in open science initiatives, academic mentoring, and exploring how music and creative expression can complement traditional therapeutic approaches.

Research Focus & Expertise

Childhood Trauma

Investigating the psychological and neurological impacts of early adverse experiences on emotional regulation, behavioral outcomes, and long-term health trajectories.

Quantitative Methods

Expertise in SPSS, AMOS, R, and JAMOVI for statistical analysis, scale development, structural equation modeling, and psychometric validation.

Trauma-Informed Interventions

Developing and validating culturally sensitive assessment tools and therapeutic approaches for high-risk and marginalized populations.

Substance & Addiction Research

Exploring the psychological factors in substance abuse, with particular attention to trauma histories and co-occurring mental health conditions.

Academic Journey

Ranked 3rd in MSc Psychology at Bengaluru City University. Currently expanding methodological expertise at University of Sussex's THRIVE Lab.
