Research Interests

My research focuses on the psychological impacts of childhood trauma, particularly its influence on emotional outcomes and negative behavioural and health outcomes. I employ quantitative research methods with a focus on SPSS, AMOS and R for statistical analysis. Prospective areas of inquiry include substance and illicit drug abuse, moral injury, trauma, coercion and violence, with an emphasis on trauma-informed and culturally sensitive applications. My work seeks to inform both psychological theory and clinical practice, with long-term contributions to research, assessment, and intervention development for marginalized and high-risk populations.
